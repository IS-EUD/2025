At least one author for each accepted paper or poster must purchase a Registration before May 29, 2025 The event has the following prices (all prices are in Euros and include VAT):

Fees Early Registration (by May 29, 2025) Late Registration (after May 29, 2025)
Regular Registration 390 € 490 €
Student Registration 270 € 350 €

In the registration system the prices are a combination of registration cost that is subject to 19% VAT (in German Umsatzsteuerpflichtig) and registration cost not subject to VAT (in German nicht Umsatzsteuerpflichtig) due to German tax reason. On the registration you pick your category (regular or student) and then the additional item is added automatically. The total price of both parts is the price shown above and it can only booked together.

All the conference registration options include access to all elements of conference including Workshops, Doctoral Consortium, Social Event, and Lab Tour.

If you have different needs (e.g., group registration, invoice with wire transfer), please contact us directly at iseud2025@ubicomp.net
